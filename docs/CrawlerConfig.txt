CrawlerConfig    爬虫任务配置
    baseUrl    站点URL

    libPath    扩展库路径

    thread    线程数

    pageProcessorProcessScript    PageProcessor process执行脚本,脚本内置属性:AUTO_CRAWLER$page

    pageProcessorSiteScript    PageProcessor site执行脚本,脚本内置属性:AUTO_CRAWLER$site

    beforeStartScript    启动前执行脚本,用于添加pipeline等操作,脚本内置属性:AUTO_CRAWLER$spider
